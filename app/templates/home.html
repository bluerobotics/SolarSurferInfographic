The SolarSurfer is a solar-powered, robotic marine vessel built with a surfboard, off-the-shelf components, and open-source hardware and software. It is designed to cross oceans, measure ocean data, and share that data with the world. The first SolarSurfer is currently on a mission from Los Angeles to Monterey, CA.
